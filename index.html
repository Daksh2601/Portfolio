I started where most engineers do — fascinated by how networks work, driven to understand every layer of the stack.

That curiosity became expertise. Through formal training at Loyalist College, hands-on field work, and deep immersion in enterprise environments, I moved from configuring initial devices to architecting multi-site hybrid cloud infrastructure that organizations depend on daily.

At Konverge Technologies, I stopped thinking in tasks and started thinking in systems. I led a full MDM platform migration to Azure Intune, implemented enterprise-wide zero-trust authentication with Windows Hello for Business, and designed hybrid Azure VPN architectures that connect on-premises operations to cloud-native services — all while maintaining the kind of uptime that makes infrastructure invisible.

I operate at the intersection of cloud architecture, network engineering, and cybersecurity. My goal isn't to keep systems running — it's to build systems that don't need to be kept running.
